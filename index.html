Frequently Asked Questions

How is this different from Juniper Square or Yardi?

Juniper Square is a CRM for LP relationships. Yardi is for accounting. Antralio is the connective layer that moves data between systems and people automatically.

Who has to change their behavior?

Virtually no one. We connect to your existing shared folders and email. Instead of your team chasing data, data comes to your team.

Is our proprietary deal data secure?

Security is our baseline, not a feature. Antralio uses isolated data siloing to ensure your data is never mixed with other firms. Crucially, we never use your data to train AI models. We integrate with your existing SSO (Okta/SAML) to maintain strict, granular access control across your associates and operating partners.
